## Executive Summary

Mussa Coral Fest is a Media/Narrative project in the Ocean/Marine domain that produces ocean cultural festivals blending music, art, science, and storytelling. The team claims 11 events reaching 1,000+ people and 80+ artists at their first anniversary. They aspire to integrate Web3 tools (digital collectibles, on-chain cultural records) to preserve ocean stories and community participation. While the team claims 20+ years of combined experience in coral reef conservation and event production, the proposal relies heavily on aspirational language about blockchain integration without demonstrating concrete Web3 deliverables or measurable conservation outcomes. The project is culturally oriented and complementary to other ocean/marine projects in the round, but its DeSci relevance and evidence of impact require closer scrutiny.
