## Executive Summary

TEENTOK is a career discovery platform targeting adolescents, combining mentorship, skill-building, and AI-driven career matching. The project is in beta stage and claims to have onboarded mentors and gathered early student feedback. While the problem space (youth career guidance) is well-recognized, the proposal lacks specificity on its AI methodology, evidence of beta testing outcomes, measurable impact metrics, and any DeSci-aligned mechanisms. The claims of guiding 'millions of teens' are aspirational without supporting data.

- Results in empowered individuals and thriving communities built on purpose-driven success

- Mentors have been onboarded and early student feedback gathered
