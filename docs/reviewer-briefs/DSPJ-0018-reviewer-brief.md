## Executive Summary

Shapeshifters proposes a transmedia storytelling experience that aims to 'realign versions of reality, beyond polarization' using generative AI imagery described as 'memetic viruses.' The project is in an early script-writing phase with a Google Slides draft as the only deliverable. Claims are highly abstract and lack concrete milestones, measurable outcomes, or scientific grounding. Compared to peer projects in the Art/Culture and Media/Narrative categories, this proposal stands out for its vagueness and use of pseudoscientific language rather than substantive project planning.

## Extracted Claims

- The project will present 'the answer to life the universe and everything' and realign versions of reality beyond polarization.
- Metapossibility: if it exists as a thought, the probability it exists is non-zero.
- Metacrisis solution: can solve any problem, cannot solve all the problems all at once.
- The project will inspire change.
- Generative AI images will act as 'memetic viruses.'
- The project promotes neuroplasticity, neurogenesis, and 'DNA activation.'
